1. Services offered

Mobile Mech provides mobile repairs and emergency breakdown support across Auckland. Services may include:

  • Mobile mechanic services for cars, light trucks, campervans and motorcycles
  • Diesel engine diagnostics and repairs
  • Fleet maintenance and servicing
  • Emergency roadside breakdown assistance, excluding towing
  • Preventative maintenance for machinery and vehicles
  • General automotive repairs and servicing

Services are carried out by qualified mechanics. Availability depends on the vehicle, job type and location.

2. Bookings and appointments

  • Bookings can be made online, by phone or by email.
  • Same-day service may be available, subject to technician availability.
  • Emergency service is prioritised for urgent breakdowns.
  • Service times are estimates and may vary due to location, traffic or complexity.
  • Booked jobs may change due to delays and unforeseen circumstances.

3. Pricing and payments

  • Prices are in New Zealand dollars and include GST unless stated otherwise.
  • Diagnostic and call-out fees may apply to mobile services.
  • A quote will be provided before major work begins.
  • Payment is due upon completion unless otherwise agreed.
  • Accepted payment methods may include PayWave, credit card and bank transfer.

Quotes are estimates and may include parts, labour, miscellaneous costs and applicable taxes.

4. Cancellations and no-shows

  • Cancellations should be made at least six hours in advance.
  • Late cancellations or missed appointments may incur a call-out fee.
  • Higher rates may apply for after-hours or remote-area emergency services.

5. Warranty and workmanship

  • Work is backed by a six-month workmanship warranty unless otherwise noted.
  • Manufacturer or supplier warranties apply to parts where relevant.
  • Mobile Mech is not liable for issues caused by pre-existing conditions or misuse.
  • If a warranted part fails, the manufacturer or supplier may replace it. Labour charges may still apply for removal and reinstallation.

6. Liability

  • Reasonable care will be taken in delivering services. Mobile Mech is not liable for delays or damage caused by factors beyond its control, including traffic, weather or supplier issues.
  • Mobile Mech is not responsible for loss of or damage to personal items left in a vehicle.
  • Service and pricing information may change and is subject to availability and correction.

7. Customer responsibilities

Customers must:

  • Ensure the vehicle or equipment is in a safe and accessible location.
  • Provide accurate service history and vehicle information.
  • Authorise work before it begins.
  • Ensure the vehicle is parked on a flat, solid surface rather than grass or loose gravel.
  • Be present, or arrange for someone to be present, to hand over keys at the agreed time.

8. Website content

  • Website descriptions, images and branding are the property of Mobile Mech unless stated otherwise.
  • Content must not be reproduced or reused without permission.
  • Users must not engage in malicious or unauthorised activity on the website.

9. Privacy

Personal and contact information collected through bookings or communications should be handled in accordance with the business privacy policy. A dedicated privacy policy should be reviewed and published before launch.

10. Amendments

These terms may be updated. Changes will be published on this page and take effect from the stated publication date.

11. Booking information and technician requirements

When booking a service, provide:

  • Vehicle make, model and year
  • Vehicle registration number
  • Vehicle location
  • Requested service type
  • Preferred date and time
  • Payment details or confirmation that payment will be made directly

The vehicle must be in a safe location, on a flat surface and reasonably close to the booking address. Access to the vehicle and keys is required for the duration of the work.

12. Pricing information

Prices are intended to be current and accurate, but errors can occur. If a pricing discrepancy is identified, the customer should be contacted before the booking proceeds. Final costs may vary where the actual work differs from the initial estimate.

13. Consumer Guarantees Act

Nothing in these terms limits any rights available under New Zealand consumer law. Customers should receive services carried out with reasonable care and skill, matching their description and fit for their intended purpose.

14. Governing law

These terms are governed by New Zealand law. Any dispute will be subject to the jurisdiction of the New Zealand courts.

15. Vehicle photographs

Mobile Mech may photograph the vehicle before work begins to record pre-existing damage. The existing website states that photographs are held for six months and should not include personal or sensitive information. Confirm the intended retention period and privacy process before launch.
