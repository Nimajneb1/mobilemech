# Mobile Mech simplified rebuild

Open `index.html` in a browser to review the static site.

## What changed
- Removed the top information bar and most repeated marketing copy.
- Reduced the homepage to one hero, three service links and one contact band.
- Removed decorative card shadows and hover movement.
- Simplified the footer and inner pages.
- Kept the quote-request form as a mailto draft generator.

## Before publishing
- Confirm pricing, service details, opening hours and terms with the business owner.
- Replace any temporary or remotely hosted images with approved local assets.
- Connect a hosted form service if quote requests should be submitted through the website rather than the visitor's email app.
